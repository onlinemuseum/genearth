Efficiency

Optimization

Using less energy with equal results


  1. There is no way around it: production requires energy.
  2. Humans need, enjoy or indifferently make produce
While we always need energy, we can make production more efficient. For instance:
Old cars required more energy to drive than newer ones. In other words: transport by car has already become much more efficient.

However:
a train is circa 3x more energy efficient than a car.

So, we can:
  1. Improve our mode of travel
  2. Change our mode of travel
...to make travel more efficient.

Besides travel we could improve efficiency across many sectors: factories, administration, internet, crypto mining and more.
