Campaigning

Cathing people's attention

Start your own environmental campaign

If you are to start your own campaign, the sky is the limit really. There are so many ways to go about it, that I couldn't point you in one direction.

Yet, what I can stress, is that environmentalism truly exists in the minds of people; it is culture. Campaigning is such a strong device as it can bring change to a number of people (instead of just you).

You can only expect people to change if things are brought to their attention.


Creative Campaigning

If we compare climate activist Greta Thunberg with the initiator of Ocean Cleanup, they are both working towards the same goal. One may be more scientifically inclined, and the other may be a great speaker - they do what suits them best.

But mostly, if you really want to bring about change, you should just go out there and simply 'do'. Greta Thunberg became famous because she performed a school strike. She apparently was very angry with people's lackluster attitude toward Climate Change. Perhaps that anger fueled her hard work and bold action to quit school.
