Green Energy

Replacing fossil fuels with renewable energy


When we talk about Pollution, it often relates to our use of fossil fuels. If we were to stop emitting these fuels into earth's atmosphere, the 'Greenhouse Effect' would almost certainly be diminished.

Here are the most noteworthy types of renewable energy:



  1. Wind Power
    Wind Turbines used at the Gansu Wind Farm in China

  2. Hydropower
    The Hooverdam produces electricity with dynamos

  3. Geothermal Energy
    Icelanders warming houses with earth's heat

  4. Bioenergy
    A Stirling Engine working through 'biomass combustion'
